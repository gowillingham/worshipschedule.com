We're sorry that you are experiencing problems logging into Worshipschedule. Some reasons for this are:

Logging in with the wrong password. If you think that you've might have forgotten your password, you can recover it by clicking on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. When you select this link, you'll need to provide your user name. Then Worshipschedule will send you your current password via the email address registered in your account.

Logging in with the wrong user name. If you have forgotten your user name, you may be able to recover it from the welcome email you should have received from Worshipschedule when your account was created (although you may have changed your user name since then).

If you have changed your user name and subsequently forgotten it, you'll need to contact the Worshipschedule account administrator for your church (the person from your church who manages your Worshipschedule account) and have them send you your account credentials.
